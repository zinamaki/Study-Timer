    An open source desktop application for tracking your studying

    Project maintained by zinamaki Hosted on GitHub Pages — Theme by mattgraham

    Hard work beats talent when talent doesn't work hard

    - Tim Notke

    Study Timer tracks your study sessions with a session label and a duration. Your study history is saved into a text file to document your time. When you are struggling to find motivation, just hit the Motivation button.
